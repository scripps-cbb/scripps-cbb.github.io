Prizes are announced on **Monday at 12:30 pm** following judge
deliberations over lunch. Seven prizes are awarded:

- **Grand Prize** — the team with the highest combined score across all
  five [rubric categories](#rubrics).
- **Most Innovative** — for the most original approach or genuinely new
  technique.
- **Best Scientific Merit** — for the project tackling the most
  significant scientific problem.
- **Biggest Impact** — for the project most likely to change practice or
  unlock follow-on research.
- **Best Presentation** — for the team that communicated their work
  most clearly and compellingly.
- **Cross-Disciplinary Award** — for the project that most meaningfully
  integrated multiple disciplines.
- **Claude's Choice Award** — selected by the AI judge.

### Presentation format & eligibility

- Each team gives a **5–7 minute presentation** on Monday morning.
- **Submit your slides on Monday** to be eligible for prizes.

See the [judging rubric](#rubrics) for how projects are scored.
