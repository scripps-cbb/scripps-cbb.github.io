For questions of any kind, email
[**{{ site.event.contact_email }}**](mailto:{{ site.event.contact_email }}).
We typically respond within a few working days.

### What to include

- **Project proposals** — a short description, target outcome, and the
  skills or roles you're looking to recruit.
- **Sponsorship enquiries** — your organization, sponsorship level, and
  what you're hoping to get out of the partnership.
- **Press / coverage** — your outlet, deadline, and the angle you're
  working on.
- **General questions** — eligibility, format, accommodation, anything else.
